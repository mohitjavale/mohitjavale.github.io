More about me...

I am currently a student persuing my bachelor's degree in B.E. Mechanical from BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus.
My main interest lies in the field of robotics, which has fascinated me throughout my childhood, and yet continues to do so.

Although not narrowed down completely yet, I am particulary interested in how intelligence can be imparted into lifeless mechanical objects, enabling them to do perform seemingly difficult tasks. In the persuit for the same, I have dabbled quite a bit in traditional algorithmic methods, such as kinematical analysis, control theory, as well as DL/RL techniques, and plan to continue to explore them further through small projects of mine.
